Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, name (for billing only)
  • Payment data: Processed by Paddle. We never see your card number.
  • Server metadata: Server IP, location, creation time
  • Connection metadata: Bytes transferred (aggregate, for billing), last handshake time

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or DNS queries
  • Traffic content or packet inspection
  • Connection timestamps or source IPs
  • Any personally identifiable traffic data

Data Storage

Account data is stored in encrypted databases in EU (Germany). VPN servers do not write any traffic logs to disk. Server logs are limited to process health (uptime, memory).

Third Parties

We share data only with: Paddle (payment processing), Hetzner (server hosting). Both are GDPR compliant.

Your Rights (GDPR)

You may request data export, correction, or deletion at any time by emailing privacy@nexguard.io.
